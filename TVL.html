What's TVL Track?

The Technical-Vocational-Livelihood (TVL) Track in Senior High School is designed to provide students with practical and hands-on education that prepares them for immediate employment or entrepreneurship. This track focuses on developing industry-specific skills, technical knowledge, and vocational competencies across various fields. Students can choose from several specializations within the TVL Track, including Information and Communication Technology (ICT), Home Economics (HE), Electrical Installation Maintenance (EIM), and Industrial Arts (IA). Each specialization offers a tailored curriculum that enhances students' employability and readiness for technical careers, fostering a strong foundation for success in the workforce or further vocational training. We are offering all specializations in the TVL Track.
