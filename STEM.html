What's STEM?

TThe STEM strand focuses on the study of science, mathematics, and engineering principles. Students will learn problem-solving, critical thinking, and creativity skills through various subjects. This strand provides practical experience through hands-on projects and research, and prepares students for careers in science, engineering, mathematics, and technology.

Learnings

Here are some things you can learn about Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics (STEM):

  • Study science, mathematics, and engineering principles
  • Learn problem-solving, critical thinking, and creativity skills
  • Gain practical experience through hands-on projects and research
  • Prepare for careers in science, engineering, mathematics, and technology

Career

Job Opportunities

Scientist

A scientist is someone who conducts research to understand the natural world and develop new knowledge and technologies. They use scientific methods and tools to collect and analyze data and communicate their findings to the scientific community and the public.

Engineer

An engineer is someone who uses their knowledge of math, science, and technology to design, develop, and improve systems, products, and processes. They work in various fields, such as civil, mechanical, electrical, and computer engineering, and apply their skills to solve complex problems and create innovative solutions.

Mathematician

A mathematician is someone who studies and applies mathematical principles to solve real-world problems, develop new theories, and advance scientific knowledge. They work in various fields, such as finance, engineering, physics, and computer science, and use mathematical tools and methods to model and analyze complex phenomena

Technology Specialist

A technology specialist is someone who is proficient in the use of technology and applies their skills and knowledge to develop and implement innovative solutions in various fields. They work in areas such as information technology, cybersecurity, data analytics, and software development, and stay up-to-date with the latest trends and advancements in their field.
