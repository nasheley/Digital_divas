What's Academic Track?

The Academic Track in Senior High School is designed to provide students with a comprehensive and in-depth education that prepares them for higher education and future careers. This track focuses on developing critical thinking, advanced knowledge, and specialized skills across various fields of study. Students can choose from several strands within the Academic Track, including Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics (STEM), Humanities and Social Sciences (HUMSS), Accountancy, Business, and Management (ABM), and General Academic Strand (GAS). Each strand offers a tailored curriculum that enhances students' competencies and readiness for college courses, fostering a well-rounded intellectual foundation and equipping them with the tools necessary for academic and professional success. We are offering all strands in Academic Track except the General Academic Strand (GAS).
