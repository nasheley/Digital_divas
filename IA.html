What's IA?

The Industrial Art - Automotive strand in Senior High School teaches students to diagnose, maintain, and repair vehicles, including cars, motorcycles, and trucks. They will learn about vehicle systems and components, gain practical experience through hands-on training, and cover topics such as safety, tools, and customer service. Graduates can pursue careers as automotive technicians, service advisors, or automotive engineers.

Learnings

Here are some things you can learn in the Industrial Art - Automotive strand:

  • Learn to diagnose and repair vehicles
  • Study vehicle systems and components
  • Practice safety when working with vehicles
  • Gain hands-on training in laboratory

Career

Job Opportunities

Auto Technician

Perform routine maintenance, diagnose and repair automotive systems, and test vehicles to ensure they are running smoothly.

Auto Mechanic

Repair and maintain vehicles, identify problems and perform diagnostic tests, and recommend appropriate repairs or replacements.

Auto Electrician

Install and repair electrical systems in vehicles, such as wiring, lights, and sound systems.

Automotive Designer

Create and develop new automotive designs, using computer-aided design (CAD) software and traditional sketching techniques.
